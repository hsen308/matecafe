"Good coffee is a privilege.
Great coffee is a love language."

Born from a simple dream on the shores of Batroun, Cafe Mate Coffee was founded with one unwavering belief: that every guest deserves a cup crafted with genuine care. What began as a neighbourhood gathering spot quickly became the heart of a community — a place where friendships deepen over flat whites and afternoons disappear into the gentle rhythm of the sea breeze.

We source our beans from ethical, single-origin farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, partnering directly with growers who share our philosophy of quality over quantity. Every roast is calibrated in-house to unlock the unique terroir of each origin — a practice that demands patience, skill, and an almost obsessive devotion to flavour.

Our team of dedicated baristas are more than technicians — they are storytellers in steam and extraction. From the precise 9-bar pressure of an espresso pull to the meditative pour of a V60, every cup that leaves our counter is a deliberate act of hospitality. We are Cafe Mate. And this is our love letter to coffee.

Why Choose Us

More Than Coffee

A complete sensory experience in the heart of Batroun.

Specialty Sourcing
Every bean we serve has been traced to its origin farm, harvested with care, and selected through rigorous cupping sessions. We partner directly with growers in Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and beyond — ensuring both quality and ethical traceability in every cup.
In-House Roasting
Our head roaster calibrates every batch with obsessive precision — light roasts to preserve floral and fruit notes, medium profiles to unlock caramel complexity, and dark roasts for those who crave full-bodied intensity. Freshness is not a promise; it is our standard.
Seasonal Menu
Our menu evolves with the seasons, allowing us to highlight the finest ingredients at their peak. From summer mango smoothies to winter spiced lattes, each seasonal offering is thoughtfully composed to reflect the moment and delight our returning guests with something new.
Private Events
Host your next intimate gathering, corporate meeting, or celebration at Cafe Mate. Our venue can be exclusively reserved for private groups, with a bespoke catering menu crafted to your preferences. Contact us to discuss your vision and let us create a truly memorable experience.
